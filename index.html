Legal

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

At CAUG Innovations, your privacy is a fundamental right — not an afterthought. This policy explains how our apps handle your data.

Data We Collect

Our apps are designed with data minimization in mind. We only collect data strictly necessary for core functionality. We never sell your data to any third party.

How We Use Data

Any data collected is used solely to provide the app's core features. We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any commercial purpose beyond the app's stated function.

Third-Party Services

Some apps may use third-party services such as AI APIs or analytics tools. These have their own privacy policies. We only select privacy-respecting partners.

Data Storage

Where possible, data is stored locally on your device and not transmitted to our servers. If cloud features are used, this is clearly disclosed in the app before use.

Children's Privacy

We are committed to protecting younger users. Our apps do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13 without verifiable parental consent.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, modify, or delete any personal data we hold. Contact us at any time and we will respond promptly.
