Legal

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

At CAUG Innovations, your privacy is a fundamental right — not an afterthought.

Data We Collect

Our apps are designed with data minimization in mind. We only collect data strictly necessary for core functionality. We never sell your data to any third party.

How We Use Data

Any data collected is used solely to provide the app's core features. We do not use your data for advertising or profiling.

Third-Party Services

Some apps may use third-party services such as AI APIs. These have their own privacy policies. We only select privacy-respecting partners.

Data Storage

Where possible, data is stored locally on your device. If cloud features are used, this is clearly disclosed in the app before use.

Children's Privacy

Our apps do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13 without verifiable parental consent.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, modify, or delete any personal data we hold. Contact us and we will respond promptly.
